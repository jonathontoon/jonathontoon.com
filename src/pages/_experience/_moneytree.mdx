# Moneytree

_Note: In late 2025, Moneytree celebrated its acquisition by MUFG Bank, Ltd. and its subsidiary, WealthNavi Inc., marking a new chapter in its mission to innovate in digital finance as it prepares to launch a next-generation digital bank._

Moneytree began in 2012 as Japan's first iOS personal finance app, earning Apple's App of the Year recognition. Since then, it has evolved into a comprehensive platform serving over five million users, connecting to more than two thousand financial institutions, and supporting 120 enterprise customers. Across more than five years with Moneytree, I had the privilege to shape both the craft of design within the company and the trajectory of its business—first as a designer, and later as a member of the leadership team.

## Director of Discovery & Design
### November 2021 – July 2024

I returned to Moneytree as Director of Design and later transitioned to Director of Discovery, leading the business vertical responsible for incubating new products. During this period, I rebuilt and led, hands-on, a team of product and brand designers, establishing comprehensive hiring practices, rituals, career rubrics, and best practices for systems and research.

I also established and led the Moneytree Business team, successfully shipping a discovery-backed SMB product in just six months. This product launched at ¥2M JPY ARR and delivered significant value to small business owners, saving them 30 hours per week. Simultaneously, I led a Growth marketing team focused on removing sales bottlenecks through funnel automation and landing page optimizations, ultimately creating a self-serve API experience for enterprise prospects.

Throughout this role, I regularly collaborated with other cross-functional partners on revenue-generating initiatives. Notable projects included a personal lending discovery project with Visa that generated ¥4M JPY in revenue, and the design and launch of a new mobile product for Japan Post Bank.

## Product & Design Lead
### November 12, 2016 — July 30, 2019
I joined Moneytree as the company's second full-time designer, reporting to the Director of Product and collaborating closely with a small mobile engineering team. Together, we shipped frequent updates that measurably boosted both user engagement and revenue.

Shortly after joining, I stepped into the role of Product & Design Lead. Designing and managing a new product supporting regional banks and their customers on the Moneytree LINK platform. I owned the complete end-to-end product experience, including design, delivery, and cross-functional operations with customer-facing teams. This strategic launch unlocked an entirely new revenue stream, beginning with Japan's largest regional bank, Bank of Yokohama, followed by several additional major signings.

As the platform expanded, I continued designing foundational product primitives, common interfaces that all products built on Moneytree LINK would leverage. These included a streamlined onboarding experience, intuitive account management flows, and standardized authorization processes for financial data sharing.
