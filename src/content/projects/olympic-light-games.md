Contributed to the creation of Olympic Light Games, a series of 7 interactive online sports games developed by RESN Auckland to coincide with the London Olympics 2012.
The project featured beautiful screen design and dynamic visual effects that captured the excitement and energy of the Olympic games. As an intern, I played a swiss army knife role, contributing both production design and frontend engineering work to bring these interactive experiences to life.
The games showcased RESN's signature style of combining cutting-edge web technology with stunning visual design, creating engaging experiences that celebrated the Olympic spirit through interactive digital media.
