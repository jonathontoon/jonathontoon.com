Led the design and development of Xero Me, a mobile application focused on helping employers communicate with their employees more effectively.
Working with a team of 7, I led the mobile design for the iOS application from initial conception through to successful launch. The project involved both design and development work, implementing iOS components using Objective-C and Swift.
Key activities included:
- Complete mobile design leadership from inception to ship
- iOS component development and implementation
- User testing session prototyping and execution
- Cross-functional collaboration with engineering and product teams
The app successfully launched and provided a new channel for employer-employee communication within the Xero ecosystem.
