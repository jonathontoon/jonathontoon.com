Led design and contributed development for a innovative payment method using Bluetooth technology across iPhone, iPad, and Android devices as part of Origami's pivot to in-store payment solutions.
This groundbreaking project involved creating a completely new payment experience that didn't rely on traditional NFC technology. The solution enabled contactless payments through Bluetooth connections, making it accessible to a wider range of devices and merchant terminals.
Key contributions:
- Complete UX/UI design for the Bluetooth payment flow
- iOS development using Objective-C and Swift
- Cross-platform consistency across iPhone, iPad, and Android
- Stakeholder presentations and proposal development
- Product strategy alignment with company's merchant-focused goals
The project represented a significant technical and design innovation in the mobile payments space, offering an alternative to existing payment methods.
