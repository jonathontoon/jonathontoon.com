Product and user experience design for the onboarding of a personal banking product built in partnership with 三菱UFJ銀行 (MUFG Bank), Japan's largest bank.
This project involved designing the complete user experience for Mable, a personal finance management app that integrated with MUFG's banking services. The focus was on creating an intuitive onboarding flow that would help users understand and adopt automated money management features while maintaining the trust and security expectations of traditional banking customers.
