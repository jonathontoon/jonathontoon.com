I led the product and brand designers at Moneytree during a period of rapid customer growth, which included multiple high-stakes engagements with major Japanese banks such as MUFG and Japan Post Bank. I drove design efforts across both the consumer app—used by millions of people in Japan to manage their money—and the financial platform we built for enterprise customers. While collaborating directly with designers I also worked hands on with the CEO, Chief of Product and other executives on strategy. Along the way, I established design systems, research practices, hiring frameworks, and career rubrics that enabled us to move quickly while navigating ambiguity.
