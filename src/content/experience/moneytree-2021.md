## Overview

My journey with Moneytree spanned two distinct periods, reflecting both the company's evolution and my growing leadership responsibilities in design and product discovery.

## First Tenure (2016-2019): Product & Design Lead

Initially joined as Product & Design Lead, focusing on establishing design excellence and product strategy during Moneytree's growth phase. During this period, I worked closely with the core team to build foundational design systems and user experience patterns.

Key achievements included establishing design practices, contributing to product strategy, and helping scale the design function as the company expanded its offerings.

## Second Tenure (2021-2024): Director of Design & Discovery

Returned as Director of Design, later becoming Director of Discovery, the business vertical responsible for incubating new products.

During this time, I rebuilt and led, hands-on, a team of product and brand designers, establishing hiring practices, weekly rituals, career rubrics, and best practices for design systems and research.

I also founded and led the Moneytree Business team, shipping a discovery-backed SMB product in six months that launched at ¥2M JPY ARR and saved business owners 30 hours per week. At the same time, I led a Growth marketing team to remove sales bottlenecks through funnel automation and landing page optimizations, creating a self-serve API experience for enterprise prospects.

Additionally, I regularly partnered with other leaders on revenue-generating initiatives, from a personal lending discovery project with Visa that generated ¥4M JPY in revenue, to the design and launch of a new mobile product for Japan Post Bank.

## Impact & Legacy

Across both tenures, I contributed to Moneytree's transformation from a personal finance tool to a comprehensive financial platform, establishing design leadership practices that supported the company's growth and product innovation.
