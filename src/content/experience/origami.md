I worked with the team at Origami during its major pivot from a mobile shopping app to payment technology for stores. I collaborated directly with the founders, longside two other talented designers, to shape what we should build next. I led design and also contributed development work in Objective-C and Swift for a new Bluetooth payment method across iPhone, iPad, and Android devices. Spent time prototyping ideas and presenting to stakeholders to get buy-in for new features, a first on iOS for Japan at the time.
