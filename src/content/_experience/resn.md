My first real design job was as an intern at RESN, one of the best digital agencies in New Zealand. I did everything you'd expect from an intern: design, front-end development on projects for big clients like Google and FedEx, and even sweeping the floors. I learned a ton about balancing creative ideas with actually building things that work. The team at RESN was incredible and taught me what great design and technical collaboration look like.
